# Lumen Coffee Roasters

*Small-batch coffee, roasted with intent.*

## Our story

Lumen started as one roaster, one drum, and a stubborn belief that coffee tastes better when
you can trace it to a single farm. We still roast in small batches, still cup every lot before
it ships, and still write the tasting notes ourselves — no committee, no focus group. Six
origins, rotated as harvests allow, roasted to let each one taste like where it came from.

## Voice

> Warm, craft-obsessed, no fluff. Short sentences. Talk like the person who roasted the beans,
> not a marketing department. Never oversell.
