Lumen Coffee Roasters

Our story

Lumen started as one roaster, one drum, and a stubborn belief that coffee tastes better when you can trace it to a single farm. We still roast in small batches, still cup every lot before it ships, and still write the tasting notes ourselves — no committee, no focus group. Six origins, rotated as harvests allow, roasted to let each one taste like where it came from.

Voice
